  1. trait EconomicActor extends Actor with ActorLogging with PromiseMakingActor with CounterpartyActor

    Base trait defining the behavior of all EconomicActor actors.

    Base trait defining the behavior of all EconomicActor actors.

    We follow in the tradition of Hyman Minsky and Perry Mehrling and view every EconomicActor in our framework as a kind of "bank," in the sense that the primary objective of every such actor is to monitor the balance between its inflow and outflow of payments.

    Every EconomicActor is a PromiseMakingActor and communicates with other such actors using the actor communication language defined in the acl package.

    Every EconomicActor is also a CounterpartyActor and communicates directly with ContractActorLike actors and indirectly with other CounterpartyActor actors using ContractActorLike actors as communication "channels."
